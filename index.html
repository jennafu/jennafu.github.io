About Me

I'm a new graduate from the University of Toronto with a degree in Applied Statistics and Economics. In school, most of my courses mainly are focused on statistical theories and descriptive modelling. In terms of technical skills, I have a very solid foundation in quantitative modelling and programming languages and statistical tools, including Python, R, SQL and various visualization software (Tableau, Power BI).

For the past year, I have been working at a digital health company as a data analysis specialist, where I was mainly involved in building automated dashboards and infographics to monitoring key metrics of our company's mobile app. During my time there, I was especially intrigued at how data generated from customer's interaction with the platform could have such strong story-telling abilities. This experience made me want to pursue a career in the domain of product analytics.
